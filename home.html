Our Story

Ramune Café is more than just a café; it's a portal to a bygone era of pixelated wonder and whimsical joy. Our journey began when our founder, a passionate gamer and lover of all things pixel art, decided to combine two of life's greatest pleasures: delicious cakes and aromatic coffee, with a pixelated twist. Inspired by the vibrant world of retro video games and the effervescent Ramune soda, Ramune Café was born.

The Aromatic Brews

Pairing our pixel-perfect creations with exceptional coffee is our forte. Our baristas are passionate about their craft, ensuring that every cup of coffee served at Ramune Café is a masterpiece. From silky lattes to bold espressos, we've got your coffee cravings covered. And if you prefer something cooler, our Ramune soda-inspired beverages will tickle your taste buds with effervescence and nostalgia.

A Whimsical Atmosphere

Step inside our café, and you'll be transported to a world where pixels come to life. Our décor is a delightful blend of vintage arcade cabinets, pixel art murals, and cozy nooks that invite you to relax and reminisce. Whether you're a solo adventurer or gathering with friends, Ramune Café is the perfect place to relive your favorite gaming memories while sipping on your favorite brew.

Community and Beyond

We believe in the power of gaming to bring people together, and that's why we host regular gaming nights and events for our vibrant community of retro enthusiasts. Join us for a game of classic Tetris, Mario Kart tournaments, or simply share your favorite gaming stories with fellow patrons.

Cake and Pastry Extravaganza

Our cake and pastry selection at Ramune Café is a delightful showcase of creativity and taste. Indulge in our signature Pixel Cake, a multi-layered masterpiece that replicates the pixelated landscapes of your favorite games. For a burst of flavor, try our Tetris Tart, where each colorful block represents a different fruit filling, creating a harmonious symphony of taste and texture. And don't forget to explore our ever-changing seasonal pastries, where our chefs draw inspiration from classic games and Kyoto's seasonal bounty to craft unique treats. Whether you're a devoted gamer or simply a lover of exquisite sweets, Ramune Café promises a culinary adventure like no other.
